About

Our passion lies in manufacturing a range of paper bags and gift bags that find application across sectors. These bags are used as carriers or for packaging, by our esteemed clients for variety of goods such as apparel, footwear, jewellery, watches, cosmetics, furnishing, beverages, confectionery, etc.


We customise our products for clients as per their requiremens. The customization options include choice of paper, shapes, finishes, handles, etc.


Extreme dependence on plastic and other synthetics are threatening to cause irrepairable damage to our delicate eco system. At such a junture, we at Eco Green Bags, Patna aim to restore the balance in a small yet effective way.
